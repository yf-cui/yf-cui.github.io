About

I'm working as a postdoc researcher in MLab directed by Prof. Ted-Wei Kuo, Prof. Chun Jason Xue and Dr. Nan Guan, CityU HK. I also work closely with VISAL directed by Prof. Antoni B. Chan. Prior to that, I obtained my PhD in computer science in CityU HK, MS in Telecommunications in HKUST and BE in Shandong University.

I also lead the AI research lab in Bingli Tech, Guangzhou.

My research interests include

  1. Bayesian deep learning with applications in medical images and Histopathology.

  2. Light-weight neural network and Embedded AI.

  3. Data engineering - data compression and learned index.

  4. Storage - optimization for flash reliability.
